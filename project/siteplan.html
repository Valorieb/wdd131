Site Plan - Bloom and Gather

Name

Bloom and Gather Cut Flower Garden Planner

This name reflects the purpose of the site, which is to help you grow your own cut flower garden

Purpose

To help the user plan and grow their own cut flower garden by providing information on what kinds of flowers to plant and how to care for and arrange them.

Scenarios

  • What kinds of flowers should I grow for a cut flower garden?
  • What are the care instructions for growing the most blossoms?
  • What flower combinations do I want in my bouqet?
  • How do I arrange my cut flowers?
  • What flowers do I need to plant to have blooms the entire growing season?
  • When do I start planting which flowers?
  • When do I start cutting? How much do I cut?
